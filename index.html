What is hmpstr creating?

Cosmos

Cosmos will be a text-based survival game in which you are the general-president of a planet whose galaxy is about to be consumed by a black hole. You must escape the only home you've ever known and explore the far reaches of space, in order to continue life as we know it!

Use your citizens and the resources you're able to gather to create starships, freighters, and more to traverse the treacherous void of space. Build up your arsenal and construct a base on one of many celestial bodies to choose from.

However, be warned: your people are not the only ones seeking refuge, as other players and rogue nations will align themselves for all-out war over every cubic of breathable air, every square of soil, and every drop of drinkable water, not to mention the materials you'll need to build up, repair, and upgrade your fleet.

If you manage to establish a new home (if you so choose), either through treaties with fellow planetary orphans or by crushing the residents of your target home, your work will still not be done, for there's another force on the outskirts of the galaxy, and it has no intention on leaving survivors in its wake....
