What is hempster creating?

Cosmos

Cosmos will be a text-based survival game in which you are the general-president of a planet that is no longer inhabitable. You must escape the only home you've ever known and explore the far reaches of space, in order to continue life as we know it!

Use your citizens and the resources you're able to gather to create starships, freighters, and more to traverse the treacherous void of space. Build up your arsenal and construct a base for efficient resource collection on one of many celestial bodies to choose from.

However, be warned: your people are not the only ones seeking their place in the galaxy, as other players and rogue nations will align themselves for all-out war over every cubic of breathable air, every square of soil, and every drop of drinkable water, not to mention the materials you'll need to build up, repair, and upgrade your fleet.

If you choose and manage to establish a new home, either through treaties with fellow planetary orphans or by crushing the residents of your target home, your work will still not be done, for there's still another threat in the galaxy....
